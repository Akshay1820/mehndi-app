Our Story

Born from Passion,
Crafted with Love

Nails by Pradnya began as a dream — a vision to bring luxury beauty experiences to every woman in Pimpri-Chinchwad. What started as a passion for intricate mehndi designs and nail artistry has blossomed into a full-fledged luxury beauty studio.

With over 5 years of dedicated practice, Pradnya has perfected the art of creating designs that are not just beautiful, but meaningful. Each bridal mehndi tells a love story. Each nail art piece is a tiny canvas of self-expression.

Today, Nails by Pradnya is trusted by hundreds of brides, professionals, and beauty enthusiasts across Pune for premium nail art and authentic mehndi services.

Meet the Artist

The Creative Mind Behind the Art

Pradnya

Founder & Lead Artist

◆ W

Pradnya is a self-taught beauty artist who discovered her love for mehndi and nail art at a very young age. Inspired by traditional Indian artistry and modern beauty trends, she has developed a distinctive style that blends classic elegance with contemporary design.

She has professionally trained in advanced nail techniques including gel extensions, acrylic nails, and intricate 3D nail art. Her mehndi expertise spans traditional Rajasthani, Arabic, and fusion contemporary styles — making her the go-to artist for brides across Pune.
